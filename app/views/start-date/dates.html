What are the start and end dates for John Smith's unpaid leave?

You must allow time for the worker's visa to be processed when entering a start date. If they are applying to extend their visa for the same role, the start date is the day after their current visa expires.

A person sponsored under the Government Authorised Exchange (GAE) route can work for a maximum of:

  • 12 months if under the work experience programme
  • 2 years for all the other programmes

Those already in the UK with permission on the GAE route can apply to extend their stay up to these maximum periods.
